PROFILE

Food. The necessity of Life. Fresh, Safe and Nutritious is our identity.

The world is turning towards the farm fresh fruits and vegetables it always deserved. As a valuable exporter from India, at Usha Foods we offer our customers premium quality agricultural produce round the year.

Our aim is to provide supreme agriculture farm produce characterized with safety and sanitized food at most reasonable and appropriate prices. Our motive is to enhance the consumer experience by providing healthy food for all at large volumes.
In the present time, the world is turning back towards its roots where humans demand healthy and naturally grown products for fulfilling their dietary needs. Usha Foods as a trusted supplier based in Pune, Maharashtra the heart of the agricultural belt of western India, is determined to meet the high quality and demand volumes of customers from across the globe.

We source all our products and produce from renowned, reliable and health conscious Indian Agrinet farmers and pack houses which are certified under the respected authorities of the country. We ensure benchmark quality standards for our supply which are comparable with international levels
