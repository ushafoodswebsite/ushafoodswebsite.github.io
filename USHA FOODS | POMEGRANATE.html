OVERVIEW

The onion, also known as the bulb onion or common onion, is a vegetable that is the most widely cultivated species of the genus Allium.  

We are one of the most supreme and reliable suppliers of high quality farm fresh onions from various part of India. The onions we supply are characterised with great quality, perfect taste and shape along with safe and sanitized onions. India claims to have been growing the most desirable onions around the globe. Indian Onions are demanded due to their taste, aroma, spice and textures. Red onion and Pink onions are widely farmed and exported to not only nearby countries but also to far off countries wherever the demand is present.

India is the second largest onion growing country in the world. Indian onions are famous for their pungency and are available round the year. Indian onions has two crop cycles, first harvesting starts in November to January and the second harvesting from January to May.The major varieties found in India are Agrifound Dark Red, Agrifound Light Red, NHRDF Red, Agrifound White, Agrifound Rose and Agrifound Red , Pusa Ratnar, Pusa Red, Pusa White Round. There are certain varieties in yellow onion which are suitable for export in European countries Tana F1, Arad-H, Suprex, Granex 55, HA 60 and Granex 429.
